Book overview

A collection associated with Narendra Modi’s reflections on social equality, communal harmony, and the need to overcome caste exclusion and division in public life.

Publication context

The available English edition was published in 2015, drawing on Modi’s earlier writings and reflections from before his premiership. Modi was 64 at the time of this edition.

Short summary

The text centers on caste exclusion, social equality, moral reform, and the need for a more integrated and humane public life.
