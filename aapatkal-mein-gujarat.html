Book overview

A Hindi edition of Narendra Modi’s account of Gujarat during the Emergency, centered on political resistance, underground networks, and democratic struggle.

Publication context

First published in 2004, this Hindi edition revisits the Emergency period (1975–77) through Gujarat’s resistance experience. Modi was 53 years old at first publication of this edition.

Short summary

The book revisits underground organization, documentary recollection, and the political memory of resistance in Gujarat during the Emergency.
