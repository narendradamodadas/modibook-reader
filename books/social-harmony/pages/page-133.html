Original English text

dumb at Rajkot is a living monument of kindness. To Pappaji’s
highly enlightened mind, this school was more important than
a temple. The impact of his personality reflects in his school.
Pappaji’s genuine feelings and love for this school and its
activities were immense. It would even seem that his attachment
for the school was even more than that for his family. His
approach towards the handicapped was enshrined in the
practical form of the RSS values. If the entire society takes the
responsibility of a handicapped person, it would be a great relief
to his family. There should be feeling of empathy rather than
sympathy for the handicapped and society should feel their
pain.
o
