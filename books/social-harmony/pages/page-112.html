Original English text

116
Social Harmony
mother will not emerge unless she has her child – dead or alive.
A woman is always ready to sacrifice herself for her child. This
is her strength and we bow down to it. This strength has the
capacity to cultivate virtues in society and ensure the survival
of morals and values. Our nation has been enriched by this
strength since centuries and we pray that it continues to be so
in the times to come.
