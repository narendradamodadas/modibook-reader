Let’s Create a New History
The sole aim of my life is the development of Gujarat. I
strongly believe that Sarpanches will play a crucial role in this
mission of prosperity. People will definitely remember those
who have worked for their welfare. Let us pledge to raise the
banner of prominence. Undoubtedly, we will be able to create a
new history.
