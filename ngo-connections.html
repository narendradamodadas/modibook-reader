Politicians with formal links to Christian or Muslim-run trusts, NGOs, and schools

This page is intentionally narrow and source-driven. It only includes politicians for whom I found a public, formal institutional role — such as founder, founder chairman, chairman, managing director, or chancellor — connected to a Christian-linked or Muslim-linked educational trust, NGO, university, or school. It is not a guilt-by-association list and it excludes rumors, attendance, or vague social proximity.

Method

What counts as a connection here

I counted only direct public roles on official institution pages or comparably strong source material. Christian schools are included when the politician’s role is formal and the institution is explicitly named as a school or clearly Christian-linked educational institution.

Included

  • Founder / founder chairman / chairman / managing director / chancellor roles
  • Educational trusts, minority trusts, universities, colleges, and schools
  • Christian schools such as Frank Anthony Public School and St. Joseph’s institutions

Excluded

  • Rumors or unsourced accusations
  • Mere attendance at events
  • Loose ideological sympathy without an institutional role
  • Entries whose first-year timing is not clearly sourced are shown in the table but not counted in the cumulative graph

Source notes and cautions

  1. This page does not claim illegality or impropriety. It documents formal public institutional roles only.
  2. “Christian-linked” includes Christian schools or institutions with explicitly Christian names or identities in the source set, such as Frank Anthony Public School and St. Joseph’s institutions under Jeppiaar Educational Trust.
  3. “Islamic / Muslim-linked” includes minority educational trusts and institutions whose official pages explicitly identify their Muslim-minority or Islamic institutional character, such as Darussalam Educational Trust and Jauhar University / Jauhar Trust.
  4. Where a current role is clear but the first year is not clearly stated on an official page, I left the year as unknown instead of guessing.
